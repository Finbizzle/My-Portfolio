To the left is an animaition I made for an assignemtn. I was given instruction to make an animation using the letters in my name and to make any kind of animaton. I kept it simple and had all of the letters slide around the squrae and meet up in the middle. I kept it simple because at the time of making this I was really new to animation so I didn't want to bite off more than I could chew.

To the right is the first animation project I ever made, just a green ball bouncing around. It was just supposed to be an introduction to animation but I ended up really liking the work it took to animate something. Honestly I like how it looks, the ball looks like it bounces on natural paths and the animation itself isn't super smooth but it isn't terrible either. To make both animations I used Adobe Animate using the various tools that the app has.
